About us

Welcome to Lilac Valley Farm Stay, where rustic elegance meets natural serenity. Our story begins with a dream and a vision, brought to life by Marina Ye, a passionate local interior designer. Three years ago, Marina took a leap of faith and acquired an old, run-down barn nestled in the tranquil hills of the Blue Mountains. Her plan was ambitious: to transform this weathered structure into a luxurious open-plan barn house that would offer a unique escape for those seeking a perfect blend of comfort and nature.

What is Lilac Valley Farm Stay?

Lilac Valley Farm Stay is a rustic farm stay accommodation located in the Blue Mountains. It features a beautifully restored barn house on a four-acre property, home to sheep, chickens, ducks, and a friendly goat named Rosie.

Who is the owner of Lilac Valley Farm Stay?

The property is owned by Marina Ye, a local interior designer who purchased the old rundown barn and renovated it to its current luxurious state.

What amenities are available at Lilac Valley Farm Stay?

The property offers an outdoor plunge pool, stunning cottage garden, and a relaxing atmosphere in a beautiful location in the Blue Mountains.

How can I book a stay at Lilac Valley Farm Stay?

You can book a stay at Lilac Valley Farm Stay through our website or contact us directly for availability and reservations.
