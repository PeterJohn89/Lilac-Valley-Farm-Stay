About us

Welcome to Lilac Valley Farm Stay, where rustic elegance meets natural serenity. Our story begins with a dream and a vision, brought to life by Marina Ye, a passionate local interior designer. Three years ago, Marina took a leap of faith and acquired an old, run-down barn nestled in the tranquil hills of the Blue Mountains. Her plan was ambitious: to transform this weathered structure into a luxurious open-plan barn house that would offer a unique escape for those seeking a perfect blend of comfort and nature.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide a haven where guests can immerse themselves in the beauty of the Blue Mountains, experience the unique charm of farm life, and savor a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city living. Lilac Valley Farm Stay is more than just a place to stay; it's an invitation to create lasting memories, to reconnect with nature, and to discover the magic of rustic elegance.
